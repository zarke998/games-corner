About us

We are a small group of video game enthusiasts from Serbia who decided to share their passion by making this website. Here you can find the latest news from video game industry, find new titles to play, build your dream pc config, choose beautiful wallpapers from our gallery and share your thoughts on our forum. We designed this website with dark colors to preserve one of the video gamer's most important tool, their eyes. Using dark background provides less strain to the eyes especially during the night when most gamers are active. If you want to learn more about us check out our social link and our twitch channel where we stream every day all kinds of video games.
